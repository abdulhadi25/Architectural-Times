The park is open from 10 to 6 pm. So grab your bathing suits and head over to the Dreamland Aqua Park and splash around with your kids.

VR Park

With the fast progress of technology, who doesn’t love a good old fashioned video game? Mind you, the VR Park in Dubai offers much more than simple arcade games like Pac Man. The VR Park at Dubai Mall is an amazing mix of augmented and virtual reality with a stunning effect. You and your kids can try the adrenaline pumping Burj Drop experience, where you are propelled down the side of the tallest hotel in the world, the Burj Khalifa. The VR Park doesn’t just cater to kids but to teens as well. You can navigate a spaceship to safety or kill zombies at the Walking Dead Outbreak VR game. This is a place that you should definitely not miss, and it’s great for all ages. So head down to the Dubai Mall and take in the VR experience. Trust me; it’s going to be really difficult to take your kids home after a trip to the VR Park.

Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo

Marine animals, they really are a sight for sore eyes. Dubai gives you an opportunity to explore the exotic marine life at the Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo. The aquarium actually holds the record for the World’s Largest Acrylic Panel. Spend the day surrounded by stingrays, jellyfish, giant crocodiles, sharks, and many more marine mammals and fish. The aquarium consists of a 48-meter walk through a tunnel that allows viewers to walk and take a look at animals through a glass partition. The Dubai Aquarium is one of the largest aquariums in the world.

Dubai is like a fun gold mine, and you should definitely not miss out on all the excitement. Above are just some of the places to visit in Dubai with kids, and there are a billion more. So pack your bags and head out to Dubai today.
