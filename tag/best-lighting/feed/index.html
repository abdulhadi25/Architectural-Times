]]> The lighting of a space can literally make or break a space hence it is important to choose the perfect and top lighting pieces. We have come a long way since the days of when Thomas Edison invented the first light bulb to today when we have the most Avant-Garde and aesthetically pleasing designs in lighting that seem more like works of art than simply a functional tool.

You could literally use lights as a centerpiece to enhance the beauty of your room without having to invest much on furniture. It can affect the mood of any room making it either futuristic, romantic, study-centric, majestic or glamorous. So lets look at some of the top lighting trends of 2020.  

Luckily, today we have a plethora of lighting designs to choose from, from Retro to Vintage to LED, Crystal, Copper and minimalistic.

1.Vintage lights – Top Lighting Trends of 2020

Vintage lights are back in fashion again and listed in top lighting trends of 2020. This look is perfect for a kitchen, dining area or lounge although not the best choice for a classic drawing room. If you want to give your space a hip and artistic feel then go for these retro wrought iron lights that would instantly add tremendous personality to your room. The iron rods give an industrial feel which is in perfect alignment with the modern idea of minimalistic interiors.

2. Tiffany Lights 

Bohemian and organic, each one of the Tiffany Chandeliers is handcrafted with meticulousness with its breathtaking stained glass mosaic style.

Each lamp is a collectible item with high durability and colors that never fade. If the colors look a little dull due to exposure to pollution, a little bit of oil on a soft cloth will bring back the chandelier’s vivacity. It would look great with antique or classical furniture as well as modern both.

3. LED Lights

The latest trend in LED lights is the invisible/hidden light, which evokes light at a subtle tangent angle. It is one of the most beautiful lights as it sharp but subtly incandescent. This LED light style is most suitable under staircases, windows and inside wall depressions to give the most suave look.

4. Crystal Lights – Top lighting trends of 2020

If you want your room to have a majestic and ethereal look that would bejewel it then crystals are the way to go. Crystals are feminine and always beautiful look perfect in hallways, dining rooms and drawing rooms. You can choose from glass ball pendants with their ultra glam look or grand crystal chandeliers with candlelight stands and transform your room into a royal dream.

5. Copper



Copper chandeliers have a classic and rustic old-world look that looks incredible with dark wood. It looks incredibly in synch with antique collectors items and period furniture and interiors.

6. Minimalist

If you want to make a subtle statement without screaming “look at me” this slim and simple pendant light design is the perfect option for you. The Postmodern Arch pendant has a clean and elegant look, which makes it an ideal addition to a modern designed house. This minimalistic light follows the “less is more” philosophy and looks incredible mounted above a long piece of furniture. Due to its cutting edge shape, it is one of the top lighting trends of 2020.

7. Postmodern Light


This Mediterranean Pendant Light is one of the most exuberant pieces as it uses real seashells. This seashell pendant light has a high-quality chrome finish iron frame, which can make any room seem like an underwater fantasy. This light demands instant attraction and pops the minute you walk into the room.

8. Combination Lights (LED and Postmodernist)


The Postmodern Decorative Nano Pendant Lights is one of the top most iconic lighting trends of 2020. They have an attractive mesh frame, which gives the lights its beehive/honeycomb appearance. The best part about the lights is that the wire mesh is hand-woven that gives it its natural, irregular shape and an artistic look.
