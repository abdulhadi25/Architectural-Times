]]> With the commencement of a new decade and bloom of spring, it is a conventional notion to look forward to the year’s most exciting and enthralling ventures in any domain. Just like other realms, the architectural domain is also expected to have a schedule overcrowded with high-profile fairs, exhibitions, seminars, and openings. Stereotypically, spring is an exhilarating time for the inclusive design and architecture industry. With the massive consternation and dismay, this year the spring has turned this convention upside down and bought not just bruise of blossoms rather a rapidly spreading outbreak of coronavirus that has also knocked out the world of architecture and the design corporations. Howbeit, it is worthy to investigate how the coronavirus is affecting the domain of architecture.

COVID-19 or coronavirus is responsible for accelerating and continuing to disrupt and create havoc around the world. Where it affected the ultimate activities of life, it has also thrown worse impact on the architectural events, inaugurations, building plans, seminars and much more to pen. Thence, we plumbed the nastiest effects of this deadly virus on architecture and composed its catalog. We have assembled such architectural events and ventures that are direly influenced by the coronavirus and have been deferred or utterly annulled due to the global emergency as WHO has affirmed. Further down is a non-exhaustive index of architectural occasions, programs, and preambles, converging art, architecture, and design, which have been postponed or annulled due to the prevailing pandemic (coronavirus) conditions. Here is the recent status of them,

China

  • As we all know China was the center of this epidemic disease, however, it had ultimately disrupted the life of Chinese. So, keeping healthiness and safety as the definitive main concerns, some of the most awaited architectural events in China had been enforced to postpone or absolute annul. Among them, Guangzhou’s international Furniture Fair Fair is the most prominent. The 2020 impression of this most anticipated furniture fair was fixed held on March 18-21 at The event was resolute to showcase a variety of home furniture, home decor & home textile. It is now postponed due to the affirmation of the global emergency.
  • International Design Fair, Shanghai was planned to take place from 12 to 15th March at the Shanghai World Expo Exhibition and Convention Center. Prioritizing the health and safety of teams and customers, Asia’s largest international design fair has been suspended to a new date, suspected to be held on 26 to 29th
  • He Art Museum, Guangdong opening is pondered to be the world’s most challenging architecture design. The unveiling ceremony of this complex museum (HEM), designed by the famous architect Tadao Ando, in Guangdong has been deferred from its original opening date that was March 21. The new date of the inauguration of the museum is not confirmed yet.

Italy

  • After China, Italy is now the second most prey of this fatal virus. However, in almost two weeks, the rapid deterioration and disruption are reported in Italy.  Among this deterioration, this pandemic also hit the architectural calendar of Venice. Amongst, the Venice Architecture Biennale is leading other local events and seminars. The initial date of the 17th edition of the Biennale was set to 23rd march. It is now pushed back to 29th August by organizer La Biennale di Venezia.
  • Salone del Mobile Furniture Fair or Milan is the world’s largest trade fair annually held in Milan. It is anticipated as the leading exhibition for exhibiting quality, innovation, and creativity in the furniture and design sector. Due to the epidemic outbreak of coronavirus, the 59thedition of this annual fair has been shifted to June.

Remorseful Demise of an Asset:

Humanity is infinitely demolished by this epidemic virus. The virus is prone to the loss of many lives and this index is getting amplify day-by-day. Vittorio Gregotti, the most prominent modern architect of Venice has also become prey to this pandemic. Vittorio Gregotti is an iridescent name of the architecture industry who is crowned to design many high-status buildings in Italy and worldwide. The coronavirus defeated him and his soul departed on 15th March 2020.

USA

  • Ensuring the health and safety of the team members, colleagues, exhibitors, speakers and the audience, the American Institute of Architects; AIA has taken a tough decision to postpone its almanac conference. The conference was initially expected to fetch the audience on 14-16th May, however, the decision of cancellation was made in unification with other several affairs’ cancellation.

Germany

  • Berlin Design Week 2020 is a gigantic event for Germany, giving privilege to the innovative design sector. Nevertheless, the event is a multidimensional program, embraces layouts like fairs, exhibitions, consultations, the flicks, and workshops. Due to the prompting unprecedented measures around the world, this design week is also deferred.

LONDON

  • Clerkenwell Design Week, London is pondered to be the latest architecture and design event. However, due to the coronavirus pandemic, the event is rescheduled to be held in July. The original dates of this annual design week were 19- 21st The decision of cancellation is made to safeguard the visitors and exhibitors’ health.
