]]> We are privileged to introduce the upcoming talent of our country Ms Kanwal Zaidi, who has already shown her expertise in the field of Arts. She initiated her career from Karachi and now moved to Islamabad. After completing her bachelor’s in Arts from Karachi University, she has done 4 years diploma in fine arts from Karachi school of arts and secured 1st class 1st position in Sindh Board Of Technical Education (S.B.T.E.) with painting as a major subject.

Ms Kanwal Zaidi has been participating in different group shows of painting at Expo Centre Karachi, Alliance Francaise Karachi, V.M. Art Gallery Karachi, Jharoka Art Gallery Islamabad, and Nomad Art Gallery Islamabad. Along she has attended various workshops related to arts including collaborative artwork with Residency Artists at V.M.art Gallery, Karachi. She has also attended an International Art seminar hosted by AICA Pakistan at Agha Khan University, Karachi. She has also attended Vasl workshop on critical appraisal of artists’ work by British Council Pakistan.

Some of Ms Kanwal Zaidi paintings explore perception, memory, and judgment leading to the exact definition of cognition in a magnifying experience. Working with the roots and barks of trees, she has tried to trace the acts of life through different postures of trees as a tree is very close to the human life cycle and is said to be the ‘Symbol of Wisdom’. Her versatile paintings depicting different postures have been named as beautifully broken; Patch up, Sliced, Individuality, Inner war, etc. Nowadays she is working as a freelance painter. Except that she also creates architectural and floral paintings.

Her chief motive is to achieve professional excellence in the field of art and design to obtain a position where her skills and talent can be used to the maximum potential and where challenges can diversify and expand her knowledge to contribute positively towards society at large.
