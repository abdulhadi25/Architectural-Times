]]> 2020 is all about customizing your room according to a particular theme or mood. One nifty way to do this is by creating a vision board of the kind of feel you would want your space to evoke. You can start by selecting a color palette, giving your theme a name, ascribing it a mood and/or even associating it with song or a film such as “Breakfast at Tiffany’s” or “The Great Gatsby”. Other themes that you could choose for your room are “London Chic”, “Himalayan Paradise”, “Aquamarine Fantasy” or “New York City”. It will help you select the right furniture and fittings for your room including the bed, wall hangings and best lamp for your room.

Think of your bedroom as your personal haven with your bed being the cloud that you rest upon after a long day and imagine your lamps as stars that beautify and lighten up your personal paradise. It is important to handpick a design of lamps that suits the theme of your room rather than randomly picking any lamp design.

Below, we’ve compiled a list of the most artistic and impactful lamps from our e-catalogue to help you find the best lamp for your room in 2020.

  1. Bonsai lamp.

Meet the Bonsai Table Lamp also known as the Silver Birch Light or the Silver Birch Lamp. This table lamp is one of the most sought-after lamps currently with its unique design that combines organic with tech. Its beauty is in the fact that it resembles a bonsai tree, which has fireflies sitting atop of its twigs.

The twigs are designed to be flexible meaning that you can twist and bend them into any shape. This lamp can give any room a very Zen and peaceful vibe so it is ideal for your bedroom if you have a tendency to over stress and want to set up your room as your personal unwinding zone. Finding the best lamp for your room in 2020 might end the moment you lay your eyes upon this majestic beauty. Assembling the Silver Birch Light is super easy. Twist and plug allows for easy installation and twirl and pull disassembles the lamp easily. The design allows for easy heat dissipation.

  • Telephone Booth Lamp


The Telephone Booth Lamp is ideal for those looking to go with a young and quirky theme based on the chic city vibe of London. Put up a wallpaper with black taxis, the London Eye, the Queen’s guards on your walls and voila you would instantly feel as if you’ve been transported to London. These effervescent lamps are instant conversation starters to break the ice for when you throw a party. The red color pops and adds oodles of personality to an otherwise somber room.

  • UFO lamp


If you want to give your room a futuristic/sci-fi feel a la Rick and Morty style then the UFO table lamp is ideal. It would make for a slick addition to your bedroom or study room. It would match the interior style decor that is minimalistic, modernistic and has a Jetson’s vibe. The most beautiful part about this lamp is how, in a dark room, the purple LED lights glow like a levitating space ship.

  • The Ferris Wheel

The Ferris wheel is elegant, chic and minimalistic lamp that is striking. It is a beautiful addition to your bedroom if you are into “graceful and timeless” as opposed to “loud or bold.”

Even though it is quite trendy and presents its own graceful edge. It is perfect for your bedroom if you are into earthy tones and calming shapes.

  • Butterfly Tiffany lamp

This butterfly beauty is a tree hugger’s fantasy. It is ethereal and poetic and would look ideal if your room has a “secret garden” theme. This lamp evokes a feminine and artistic charm and shines in its true glory once lit up. Since 2019 is all about being owning your individuality this lamp is worth the experimenting with.

  • Frosted Floor lamp


The frosted floor lamp is a vibrant and elegant addition to your entertainment room. Resembling, moons tied together on a thread, this glamorous floor lamp is an instant attention grabber. Go with this lamp if you want your entertainment room to leave a lasting impression.

  • Water Effect


The water effect lamp is ideal for those who are looking for a timeless piece with a natural feel. Because of its somber look, it would be suitable for a study room, a bedside table for someone with rockery or garden work in their room.

Finding the best lamp for your room in 2020 does not have to be a strenuous procedure, in fact, it can be quite smooth if you have a clear idea about what your preferences and thematic styles are. With so many beautiful options available online, the procedure is akin to a visit to an art gallery.
